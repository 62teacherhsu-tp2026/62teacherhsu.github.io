Schools Cyberfair Progress
Date: 10/18/2025
Activity theme: School cyberfair warm up ~
This year was our first time participating in the Schools Cyberfair. Our teacher guided us through understanding the competition's objectives and format. Afterwards, we browsed past teams' work to better understand how to conduct related activities.
Date: 11/01/2019~11/15/2025
Activity theme:School cyberfair work start 1 ~Decide on the competition theme and team members.
After understanding that this competition was a diverse activity, we began to look for members with different professional backgrounds and research topics. Since it was the first time participating, no one had a research topic in mind. After continuously reviewing previous works and paying attention to the people and things around us, each group finally determined the theme they wanted to explore. The first group wanted to explore the plants and animals on campus, the second group wanted to explore the school's architecture and decoration, and the third group wanted to explore the characteristics of the community.
Date: 11/22/2025
Activity theme:School cyberfair work start 2 ~ Determine the competition theme
After three weeks of group discussions, everyone focused more on the research topic and discovered that what they wanted to explore was all related to "beauty." Finally, we decided to use mosaic installation art on campus as the theme for this competition.
Date: 11/29/2025~12/06/2025
Activity theme:School cyberfair work start 3 ~ Creating Research Topic Maps
After determining the research topics, we worked in groups to create topic-level maps.
Date: 12/16/2025
Activity theme:Website Content Development Preparation Activity 1: Understanding the Basic Principles of Beauty
After determining the competition theme, we first attended a basic aesthetics class with our instructor to understand the basic principles of beauty, helping everyone to have a more unified understanding of the definition and form of beauty.
Date: 12/20/2025
Activity theme:Website Content Development Preparation Activity 2 ~ iPad Photography Skills Study
After learning the principles of aesthetics, we started practicing taking photos using iPad, and then we actually photographed the mosaic murals on campus.
Date: 12/23/2025~12/27/2025
Activity theme:Website Content Development Preparation Activity 3 ~ Continued our photography assignments
We used our weekends and after-school hours to take photos for this competition.
Date: 12/30/2025
Activity theme:Website Content Development Preparation Activity 4 ~ Focusing on the Shooting Theme
After a period of shooting, we reviewed each other's photos, and then the teacher guided us to take photos that better fit the theme of this competition.
Date: 12/31/2025~01/15/2026
Activity theme:Website Content Development Preparation Activity 5 ~ Photo editing
After the photo shoot, we started editing the photos using PhotoScape software.
Date: 01/16/2026~02/03/2026
Activity theme:Web Design
After editing the photos, we'll begin using Kompozer web editing software to divide the work into web page editing tasks.
Date: 02/04/2026~02/10/2026
Activity theme:Webpage Testing and Peer Review 1
After the webpage is completed, we check it and practice by conducting peer review.
Date: 02/11/2026~02/13/2026
Activity theme:Webpage Testing and Peer Review 2
Once the webpage content has been finalized and confirmed to be correct, it can be uploaded.
Date: 02/23/2026~03/13/2026
Activity theme:Webpage Content Check
After uploading the webpage, we double-checked the uploaded content. Everyone was happy to see the results.

Local community profile:
Ershui Township is a typical agricultural township. Due to no industrial pollution. Has a peaceful and honest atmosphere. Ershui Township is located at the southwestern tip of the gossip platform and at the top of the Zhushuixi alluvial fan plain. In the township, there is the Bagua Mountain Range, which is proud of the Changhua Plain, and Taiwan's longest Zhuoshui Stream. Ershui Township is located slightly west of the center of Taiwan Province. Located in the southeast of Changhua County. It is the southernmost township of Changhua County. The township covers an area of 29.449 square kilometers.
Summary of the case study plan:
School is where we spend most of our time apart from home. But how much do you know about your school? Have you ever felt the beauty of your school with your heart? Our Schools Cyberfair research is to hope that through our introduction, everyone will rediscover the beauty of the school and love their school more.
Brief introduction of our internet facilities:
School Internet Facilities:
Fu-Sing Elementary School currently has a computer classroom. There are 16 computers that can be used by all students. The entire school computer can access the Internet. And also open WiFi, you can wireless Internet access. It's easy for us to discuss, search and print documents in the school.
Network facilities at team members' homes:
Every team member has a computer at home. Connect to Adsl or broadband fiber optic network to access the Internet. After school time. We continued our research using email, web hard drives, and our interactive website.
Problems We Had to Overcome
First, the definition of beauty varies from person to person
The theme of our research is the beauty of the school. But because everyone's aesthetic experience is different. The definition of beauty is also different. So how to define beauty. Become our most difficult problem. Fortunately, the teacher has guided us: the basic principles of beauty. Let's do it. So that everyone does not have their own words. No consensus can be reached.
Second, photo shooting skills, poor experience
Because we have to take pictures. Showing the beauty of different aspects of the school. But we do n’t have much experience in taking pictures. So the shooting skills are not good. Fortunately, there are instructors in this regard. Teach us how to frame and take pictures. Let's get better and better at taking pictures.
Third, the hidden beauty is not easy to detect
For our group of enthusiastic but inexperienced pupils. In the beginning, only eager to participate in the event. I didn't expect to encounter a bottleneck soon. The obvious beauty, everyone soon finished shooting. And everyone took similar photos. But some hidden beauty. It is harder for everyone to notice. Fortunately, the teacher used the beauty of campus plants as an example. Guides us to look at all aspects of things from the macro perspective to the micro perspective. Only then can we successfully capture more beauty on campus.
Fourth, photo integration is difficult:
In order to clearly show the beauty of the revival campus. Everyone took a lot of photos. However, it is a big problem to fully display in the limited space of the website. Fortunately, the teacher taught us to use PhotoScape software to integrate graphics. With the assistance of a teacher. It really helped us a lot.
Fifth, the difficulty of making web pages:
Because our web production capabilities and skills are not good enough. Encountered great difficulties in making web pages. This section is guided and assisted by two teachers. Let us study step by step. The teacher guided us to learn Kompozer web creation software. Let's successfully complete the production of the web expo webpage.
Sixth, the study time is not easy to arrange:
Because the school curriculum and activities are already very compact. We can only use the daily lunch break for research. Many times we take full advantage of the convenience of computer technology. Communicate, share or exchange data using email, interactive websites, web drives. In addition, the teacher grouped us in advance. Everyone is responsible for what they are responsible for. Use the holidays to discuss with your family. I will share the information with you. So that we can better understand what we are studying. During this period, everyone's feelings became more harmonious.
Discoveries, Lessons and Surprises
◆ Interesting findings
After participating in the School Cyberfair. We personally and carefully visited our school. Only then did we discover that there are many beautiful things on our campus that we did not know. There are a lot more topics to share with family now.
◆ Learned experience
What I learned the most from participating in the School Cyberfair is that we must work in teams. Because as long as the group works together, help each other, cheer up. This will allow the ongoing project to continue. In the future, we can do it again, twice, or even ten times.
◆ Lessons learned
Originally we thought it was easy to take photos. Later, I realized that it is a hard and long learning process to take good photos and tell the beauty of the photos taken. After these studies, we can better understand the beauty around us.
◆ Surprise
We thought that our school was nothing beautiful. We only understood after participating in this online expo. It turns out that we have such a wealth of aesthetic treasures in our school. It turns out that as long as you pay more attention, in fact, life is full of surprises. So everything around you is really worthy of everyone's care and preservation!
In addition, at the exhibition of photographic works, I saw everyone's surprised and envious eyes. Then I was surprised that we can already make amazing works.

Planning elements
The combination of thematic research and courses in the Schools Cyberfair:
1. Integration with the Art Course
By investigating the beauty of campus decoration. We found many mosaic collages in the school. The teacher also guided us to create mosaic paintings using graph paper. Through the teacher's introduction, we also know the principle of beauty !
2.Integration with the Chinese Language Curriculum
The presentation of research results needs to be described in words. At this time, we can see whether the Chinese language training is solid. Through the study and organization of data. We have learned a lot of narrative grammar and the ability to integrate texts to present your point. Writing a progress report also improves our narrative and integration capabilities.
3.Integrated Learning Activities
The learning process of the entire Internet World Expo. We have to do briefings, reports, planning exhibitions, etc. These are rarely encountered in ordinary classrooms. The process requires a lot of communication, coordination and teamwork. Learn from this web expo. Let us better understand the importance of teamwork and communication.
4.Full use of computer technology
In research activities. We often use computer networks, network hard drives, emails, message boards, etc. We are learning to research and share in a more efficient and timeless manner. Let us improve our ability to use information. I also learned the skills of photo editing, web design, and photography skills.

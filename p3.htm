Research Origin
Schools are the cultural centers of the community and the birthplace of community aesthetics.Beautiful school buildings and decorations are artistic embellishments to the campus. The learning and growth benefits students gain from these architectural spaces far exceed the value of the buildings themselves. Therefore, how to let beauty flow through the campus like air, allowing every student to enjoy a wonderful life, is an important issue that schools should carefully consider when creating campus art installations. School architectural art can become a dialogue between teachers, students, and aesthetics. Now, let's explore the beauty of mosaic art on the campus of Tai Ping elementary school.

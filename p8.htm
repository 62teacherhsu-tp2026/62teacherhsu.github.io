In this era of rapid information dissemination, the best way to reach more people with your work is through online sharing. Therefore, we learn web design and related technologies to enable our work to be seen by a wider audience. Besides web design skills, we also engage in learning activities related to aesthetic principles, photo editing, and mosaic collage creation. Below are records of our various learning activities.
Aesthetics-related learning
Our theme for this year's Schools Cyberfair is the beauty of campus mosaic art. However, everyone's definition of beauty is different. Therefore, we first asked a teacher to lead everyone in explaining the basic principles of beauty so that everyone can understand and follow these principles. In addition, we also asked teachers and parents to share their creative experiences on how to take good photos.
Understanding the principles of beauty

We asked our teacher to explain the forms and principles of beauty, which gave us a foundation for defining beauty and helped us better understand how to appreciate it. This, in turn, enabled us to take better photos for our websites.
Exploring Mosaic Art on Campus
  
  
We followed our teacher to explore the mosaic murals in various corners of the campus.
